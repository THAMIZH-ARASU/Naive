For Loop:


Syntax:

For <elem_name> = <start_value> to <end_value> Step <Step_value> then <expression>

Example:

Elem fact = 1
Elem num = 11
For i = 1 to num Step 1 then fact = fact * i


While Loop:

Syntax:

While <condition> Then <expression>

Example:

Elem i = 1
While i < 10 Then i = i + 1
